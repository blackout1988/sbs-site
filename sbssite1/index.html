ABOUT US

7TH BLOCK SOCIETY is a Georgian music collective — electronic music beyond clubs, integrated into life, spaces and mindset.

We don't stick to one sound. Our space is open to every rhythm, every mood, every frequency that moves people — House, Deep House, Techno, Minimal, Organic, Melodic and everything in between.

Music • Space • Talk — three words that define everything we do. We create sounds, explore spaces and speak the language of music.
